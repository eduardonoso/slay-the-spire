How to Play

⚔️ Cards — Click cards to play them. Attack cards need a target (click an enemy). Skills play instantly.

🔴 Energy — Each card costs energy (red circle). You get 3 per turn.

🛡️ Block — Reduces incoming damage. Resets each turn.

👁️ Intents — Enemy actions shown above them. Plan accordingly!

⏭️ End Turn — Click or press E when done playing cards.

🗺️ Map — Choose your path: fights, shops, rest sites, events, and more.

⌨️ Shortcuts — 1-5 select cards, E ends turn, Escape cancels.
